A Simple Bookmark Manager

A clean and a simple interface to organize your favorite websites. Open a new browser tab and see your sites load instanly. Try it for free.



Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your favorite websites. Your bookmark sync between your devices so you can acces them on the go.

Simple Bookmarking Speedy Searching Easy Sharing

Bookmark in one click

Organize your bookmarks however you like. Our simple drag-and-drog interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favorite sites.

Intelligent shearch

Our powerful search feature will help you find saved sites in no time at all. No need to trawl through all of your bookmarks.

Share your bookmarks

Easily share your bookmarks and collections with others. Create a shareable link than you can send at the click of a button.
